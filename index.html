About me

Hej! I'm a software engineer based in Sweden.

My weapon of choice is Haskell, but I also regularly get hurt messing with C, TypeScript, and Python.

I earned a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Chalmers University of Technology, where I conducted my research under the guidance of Alejandro Russo. Before that, I received a Licentiate Degree in Computer Science from the National University of Rosario.

I joined DPella in 2021, a start-up commited to developing correct-by-construction tools that allow computing privacy-preserving analytics over sensitive data using Differential Privacy.

Sometimes I also write about random stuff.
