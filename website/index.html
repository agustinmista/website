About me

Hej! I'm a software engineer based in Sweden.

My weapons of choice are Haskell and Nix, but I also have experience with a variety of other languages and tools. My professional interests include automated testing, programming languages design and implementation, digital electronics, low-level programming, web development, among many others.

I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Chalmers University of Technology, where I conducted my research under the guidance of Alejandro Russo. Before that, I received a Licentiate Degree in Computer Science from the National University of Rosario.

I'm currently focused on developing reproducible software environments at Zenseact. Before that, I worked at DPella, helping develop their first pilot solution.

I also write about random stuff. Not as much as I'd like, though.
